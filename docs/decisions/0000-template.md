# ADR NNNN: Title

- **Number**: NNNN
- **Title**: short noun phrase that summarises the decision
- **Status**: Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-NNNN
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD

## Context

What is the situation that forces a decision? What constraints are
in play? What did we know at the time? Avoid jumping to solutions
here; this is the framing.

## Decision

What we chose. State it plainly in one or two sentences before
expanding. Should be readable in isolation as "the answer".

## Alternatives considered

- **Option A** — one-line summary and one-line reason it was
  rejected.
- **Option B** — same.
- **Option C** — same.

At least two alternatives. If only one option existed, the decision
probably did not deserve an ADR.

## Consequences

What this implies day to day. Both the benefits and the costs:
operational toil, edge cases that now matter, future work this
forecloses, future work this enables. Be honest about the trade
offs.
